Summary: Max Temperature

Temperature and Latitude have a strong relationship. The lower you get in latitude, the higher the temperature. The warmer temps in the southern hemisphere is due to tilt of the earth this time of year. In the Latitude vs temperature scatter plot, you can clearly see an almost horsehoe shape, clearly showing the further you go away from 0 or the equater, the more extreme the temps.
